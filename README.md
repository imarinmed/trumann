# Trumann - AI-Powered Career Management Platform

> **Transform your job search with AI-driven intelligence, automation, and enterprise-grade features.**

Trumann is a comprehensive career management platform that revolutionizes the job search experience through artificial intelligence, intelligent automation, and data-driven insights. Built with functional Swift and privacy-by-design principles, it provides everything from resume optimization to interview preparation and application tracking.
